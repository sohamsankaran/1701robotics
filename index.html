1701 Robotics

assistive robots for dirty jobs

Robots that matter

Solving hard problems in unsexy industries

1701 Robotics is a team of Yale Engineers and Computer Scientists working on assistive robots for dirty jobs. We believe that robots have tremendous potential in dangerous and volatile work environments that are often ignored by startups due to lack of familiarity. We're currently concentrating on the construction industry.

Right now, we're working a "follow-me" robot which can carry a few hundred pounds of stuff. We're also in the early stages of building a robot for fully automatic surveying, staking and indoor layout drawing.
